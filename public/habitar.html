Service

Habitar is Handled's AI-assisted home furnishing service. In under 72 hours, we turn an empty floor plan into a concrete buying proposal: curated furniture, real prices, direct links to stores, and multiple options per room. The client chooses. We've already done the thinking.

The deliverable isn't a mood board — it's an actionable document you can shop from the next day.

01

Context

Moving into or renovating a home is one of the most expensive and confusing processes a person faces. The furniture market is fragmented: dozens of stores (Easy, Frávega, MercadoLibre, Ciudad Muebles, Magnolias Deco, Almadera, and more), each with hundreds of options, prices that change by promotion, and uncertain stock.

The person moving in isn't an interior designer. They know they want their home to look good, have a rough budget in mind, and simply don't have the time or framework to compare 40 dining tables on their own. The typical result is impulse purchases, inconsistent styles, and a budget that runs out halfway through.

Handled solves that by applying AI as a curation layer: we process the client's brief, analyze real options in the local market, and build a coherent, visual, and budgeted proposal — ready to act on.

02

Problem

Furnishing an apartment from scratch means making dozens of purchasing decisions in parallel, with no clear picture of how the space will look or what the total will cost. These are the friction points Habitar solves.

01
No idea where to start

An empty space is paralyzing. Without a clear priority order per room or a defined style direction, people either postpone indefinitely or buy at random — and regret it later.

02
Can't visualize the result

A piece of furniture in a catalog photo says nothing about how it will look in a specific room. Without a visual reference of the assembled set in a similar space, every purchase involves a leap of faith.

03
Opaque budget

There's no single place to see the full estimated cost of furnishing a home by room. People accumulate purchases with no sense of the running total until the budget is gone halfway through.

04
Hours lost searching

Comparing options across multiple stores takes hours. Every platform has its own filter logic, its own delivery timelines, and its own stock conditions — with no unified view across any of it.

05
Aesthetic inconsistency

Without a curation filter, it's easy to end up with a Scandinavian table, industrial chairs, and a modern sofa that don't speak to each other. The space looks fine in parts but has no identity as a whole.

06
No support for the purchase

Coordinating deliveries from multiple stores, tracking stock, managing changes, and organizing assembly are tasks no one owns — and they fall on the person who already has a hundred other things to handle.

03

Solution

Habitar runs in two phases. The first turns the client's brief into a concrete buying proposal. The second supports the execution: final selection, purchase coordination, and delivery tracking.

Core deliverable
A room-by-room furnishing proposal with style options, real prices, and direct links to every product.
→
Phase A — Curation
Furnishing Proposal

From the intake form, Handled processes the brief with AI, maps the rooms to furnish, defines a coherent aesthetic direction, and selects real options from the local market. The result is a navigable presentation, organized by room, with 2 to 3 options per category.

  • Brief analysis: rooms, style references, total and per-room budget
  • Curated furniture selection with direct store links (Easy, Frávega, MercadoLibre, specialty stores)
  • 2 to 3 options per room with style, price, and visual reference
  • Estimated budget per room and consolidated total
  • Proposal delivered within 48 to 72 hours of intake
Phase B — Support
Purchase & Delivery Coordination

Once the client confirms their choices, Handled manages the buying process: verifies stock, coordinates orders across stores, and tracks deliveries until everything arrives. Optional — quoted separately based on complexity.

  • Stock and availability check at time of purchase
  • Order coordination across multiple stores
  • Delivery tracking and issue management
  • Direct WhatsApp communication throughout the process
Exclusions — Not included in scope
Interior design Floor plans Renovation or construction Technical lighting Décor & accessories Furniture assembly

02

Strategic Objectives

Conversion

The current booking flow — WhatsApp message, manual confirmation, manual entry — does not scale. Every reservation requires direct team intervention. The primary objective of the site is to eliminate this bottleneck by integrating SigueFit as the default booking engine, so clients can discover, select, and reserve a class without any back-and-forth. The site is not a brochure — it is a sales channel.

Positioning

Attic has no owned digital presence beyond Instagram. For a prospective client who doesn't arrive through a direct referral — someone who finds the name through Google, a collaboration, or press — there is nothing to land on. The website needs to close that credibility gap immediately. Every design and copy decision should communicate quality, intention, and expertise from the first scroll. The instructor team and the variety of disciplines are the core proof points.

Audience Expansion

The current client base is predominantly women aged 20–30. The studio wants to actively grow into a male and high-performance athletic segment — a demographic that typically evaluates differently: they respond to technical credibility, concrete results, and a tone that is athletic rather than lifestyle-oriented. The site needs to speak to both audiences without diluting either. Visual language, copy tone, and class framing must be calibrated to bridge that gap.

Information Architecture — Site Map

Proposed structure for a clean, conversion-focused, mobile-first website.

/   Home — Visual impact, value proposition, and booking CTAs
/classes   Reformer, Barre, Cadillac, Yoga — group vs. private explained
/team   Instructor profiles to build authority and trust
/pricing   SigueFit integration for packs and class booking
/contact   Interactive map of Palermo Soho + WhatsApp for direct inquiries
  • 01 Home: Visual impact with professional studio photography, value proposition (Attic in 3 sentences), and clear booking CTAs.
  • 02 Classes: Detail on Reformer, Barre, Cadillac, and Yoga. Explaining the differences between modalities is a key client requirement.
  • 03 Team: Instructor profiles to build authority and trust — identified by the client as a core differentiator.
  • 04 Pricing / Booking: SigueFit integration for pack purchases and class reservations.
  • 05 Contact / Location: Interactive map of Palermo Soho and direct WhatsApp link for specific inquiries.

Key Differentiators — Copy Points

More Than Pilates

The inclusion of personalized Cadillac and Barre positions Attic a step above competitors in technical breadth and variety.

Adaptability

The team adapts to each student — not the other way around. This is the declared differentiator and must be present in the copy across all key sections.

Location as Lifestyle

"The heart of Palermo Soho" is not just a coordinate — it is part of the lifestyle the client wants to project. Use it as a claim in the hero and contact sections.

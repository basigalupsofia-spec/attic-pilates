Scope

Website creation for Attic Pilates — a complete owned digital presence built from scratch, structured in two phases: a full informational site (Phase A) and a proprietary booking system (Phase B).

01

Context

Attic Pilates is an active pilates studio with a defined visual identity, an established client base, and a consistent presence on Instagram. The business operates well and sustains steady demand — however, its primary digital communication channel, and practically its only one, is Instagram.

All client-facing information lives there: class schedules, pricing, modalities, bookings, and contact. The studio currently has no owned digital presence beyond social media.

02

Problem

Relying exclusively on Instagram introduces structural friction that affects both the client experience and day-to-day studio operations across six distinct areas.

01
Information Hard to Find

Instagram is not built for structured information. Schedules, pricing, and class types get buried in posts or highlights that users frequently miss — and must be updated manually with every change.

02
No Owned Entry Point

When someone searches for the studio on Google — or hears the name for the first time — there is nothing to find. Without a website, there is no search presence and no asset to capture that intent.

03
Limited Credibility

In the wellness sector, first impressions are decisive. The absence of a website in 2026 creates a trust gap, particularly for prospective clients not arriving through direct referral.

04
Third-Party Dependency

The studio's visibility and reach are subject to Instagram's algorithm, policy changes, and organic reach fluctuations — all entirely outside the studio's control.

05
No Conversion Channel

There is no owned destination to direct paid traffic, press coverage, or collaborations. Every marketing action routes to a social media profile — not a studio-owned asset.

06
100% Manual Booking Flow

The current process: a new client contacts via WhatsApp → the studio manually confirms → manually registers in the system. Every booking requires manual team intervention — a bottleneck that does not scale.

03

Solution

Creation of the complete Attic Pilates website: a fast, well-designed, owned digital space that functions as the studio's central hub — from a visitor's first impression through to class booking. The project is structured in two sequential phases.

Phase A — Foundation
Full Website

The informational, brand, and acquisition layer. A responsive, mobile-first site that establishes Attic Pilates' owned digital presence.

  • Responsive website design and development (mobile-first)
  • Sections: value proposition, classes & schedule, about the studio, contact
  • On-page SEO — structure and metadata for Google visibility
  • Visual alignment with studio identity: typography, color palette, tone
Phase B — Owned Infrastructure
Proprietary Booking System

Replacement of the current manual flow with a native booking engine built directly into the studio's own site — eliminating dependency on third-party platforms.

  • Client-facing panel: view, book, and manage classes
  • Studio admin panel: schedules, classes, availability
  • Package and class credit management
  • Elimination of the WhatsApp → manual entry flow
Exclusions — Not Included in Scope
Editorial Blog E-Commerce / Store Social Media Management Domain Registration Hosting
